Science teacher Ryland Grace (Ryan Gosling) wakes up on an interstellar spacecraft light-years from home with no memory of his identity or how he arrived, only to discover he is the sole survivor of a suicide mission to save Earth from extinction.  As his memory slowly returns, he learns that a microorganism called astrophage is consuming the Sun's energy, causing it to dim and threatening a global ice age within 30 years. 

Grace must use his scientific knowledge to solve the riddle of the astrophage while stranded in space, where he eventually makes first contact with Rocky (James Ortiz), a five-legged alien from a distant solar system who faces the same cosmic threat.  Together, they develop a plan to breed a new organism, the Taumoeba, to control the astrophage population and refuel the Hail Mary, ultimately saving both Earth and Rocky's home world, Erid. 

The film, directed by Phil Lord and Christopher Miller and released on March 20, 2026, is based on the 2021 novel by Andy Weir and features a supporting cast including Sandra Hüller as Eva Stratt and Milana Vayntrub as Olesya Ilyukhina.
